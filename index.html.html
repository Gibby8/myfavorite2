About

my favorite hobby is speed cubing. speed cubing is trying to solve a rubiks cube as fast as you can. the feeling of getting a new pb or finally learning a new algorithm just fills your brain with dopamine. but learning it can be hard. The beginners method requires you to learn 4 algorithms which are string of face turns on a cube. The method that is most commonly considered the fastest cfop requires 78 algorithms.
